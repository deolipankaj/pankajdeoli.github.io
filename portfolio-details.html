Project information

  • Category: Elite-Karting Championship
  • Location: Bhopal, India
  • Project date: 23 February, 2015 to 28 February, 2015

Project details

  • The project was part of a national level Go-Kart championship named "Elite-Karting championship" organized by Elite-Techno groups. The aim of the project was to build a Go-Kart from scratch and compete nationally.
  • Although this was our university's first student event, we were excited. We built a team of 12 students who had exterpise in their specific fields and contributed greatly in acheiving promising resutls during the event.
  • The event (which was a 5 day event) took place in Bhopal, India in 2015.
  • Around 75 teams competed nationally for various spots throughout the event.

Team Organization and Responsibilities

  • Within the team, I was responsible for tasks such as Vehicle Dynamics, Material procurement and External styling of the vehicle. Alongside me, vineet kumar pant, Aman Dhyani had an active role to play assisting me and moving forward as a Team.
  • Ankit Pundir and vineet kumar pant also headed the simulation department which was responsible for designing and performing all the simulations in CATIA and ANSYS.
  • Saurabh Khanduri and Ronit Sukhija headed the role of Team Captain and Team racer respectively. Saurabh's decision taking skills and Leadership qualities landed him in Indian Navy which the team is proud of.
  • Probably, one of the most important members was Randhir Kumar Sharma whose expertise in Manufacturing and Craftmanship motivated us everyday to work towards this awesome built.
  • Apart from this, there were more team members whose curiosity and learning nature motivated us to constantly move ahead.
